Grace Richel Tijam

Editor-in-Chief
Grace Richel "Rich" Tijam is the former NCR Governor and Acting National President of the Association of Law Students of the Philippines. Ms. Tijam is a scholar from Highschool, College, to Law School. She obtained her Bachelor's Degree in Mass Communication in the Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Maynila. She is also a Caritas Manila Scholar. Rich is a Writer, Producer, Director, Teacher, Coach, and Digital Marketing Manager by profession. She worked for over a decade in top media companies such as CNN Philippines, ABS-CBN, TV5, TFC, and many more. She also worked as a paralegal for a Law Firm in the United States, Villasis and Partners Law Firm, and Bar Review Center. Currently, she is a Consultant and an Editorial Writer at one of the leading newspaper and media outlets in the Philippines and Los Angeles. She also teaches the following subjects: 21st Century Literature, Public Speaking, Creative Writing, Copywriting, and Digital Marketing. Ms. Tijam also handles Digital Marketing Accounts for foreign clients. Also, she is working with a team for a pioneer legal platform in the Philippines.

Nestor Lim

Deputy Editor
Nestor is an IT solutions provider, people manager, community manager, educator, and blogger/writer. He pioneered and/or founded several IT service organizations in the Philippines. He led and managed numerous tech teams of over a hundred employees. He has served customers across the globe (North America, South America, Europe, Asia, Oceania) and is a staunch advocate of financial literacy and independence. Mr. Lim has a bachelor’s degree in Information Technology and a master’s degree in Information Systems and has completed coursework on a master's degree in public administration. He is currently undergoing the Juris Doctor degree program at the University of Makati School of Law.

Mark Joshua Mina Fabula

Associate Editor
Mark Joshua “Jazz” Mina Fabula is from Romblon. She worked as a Team Manager for one of the BPOs in the country. She graduated from Arellano University with a degree in Bachelor of Arts in Psychology. She was a former Managing editor, Feature writer, and News writer of The Hill View school paper. She was a Champion in English news writing in the Division Schools Press Conference in Romblon High School. She was also the Class Valedictorian in 2009.
